# Privacy Policy - FitNova

FitNova ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- Personal information such as name and email address
- Health and fitness data such as height, weight, activity, and goals
- Device data such as device type and operating system
- User-generated content such as notes, images, and feedback

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

- Provide personalized fitness and diet recommendations
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Enable AI-based insights and suggestions
- Maintain app security and performance

## 3. Data Storage And Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

- Google Fit for fitness data
- AI services including Groq API and OpenRouter

These services may process your data according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when necessary to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

## 6. User Control

You can update or delete your data at any time through the app settings.

## 7. Children's Privacy
